Fullbright College Partners Bond Over Lunch During Intramurals


The Fullbright College Intramurals 2023 event has been an exciting time for students to come together and compete in various sports. However, it has also been a time for partners to bond over lunch. During the Intramurals event, many partners of participating students gathered at Fullbright College to enjoy a lunch together. The lunch provided an opportunity for partners to meet each other, socialize, and support their significant others. The Fullbright College Intramurals Committee commends the partners for their support and involvement in the event. Fullbright College Hosts Open House Exhibit to Showcase Courses and Innovations


Fullbright College open house exhibit to showcase the latest innovations from its Bachelor of Hospitality and Tourism (BHT), Electronics and Communications Technology (ECT), and Education (EDUC) programs. The exhibit provide an opportunity for students and the community to learn more about the college's programs and the cutting-edge technologies being developed by its students. Visitors have seen displays and exhibits showcasing the latest projects developed by students from the BHT, ECT, and EDUC programs. These include innovative hotel management systems, wireless communication systems, microcontroller applications, digital signal processing technologies, and educational software applications, among others. The Fullbright College departments showcase the innovations of their students and to provide an opportunity for the community to learn more about their programs. The departments encourage all visitors to come and explore the exhibit, meet the students who developed the projects, and learn more about Fullbright College's commitment to promoting innovation and technological advancements in various fields. Fullbright College is proud of the students who have developed these innovative projects and is grateful for the support of the community. The college looks forward to more opportunities to showcase the achievements of its students and to promote excellence and innovation in various fields.

Fullbright College Celebrates Unity and Talent through Dance, Band Exhibition, and Cheer Dance Competition


Fullbright College recently hosted an exciting event that showcased the talents of its students from the Bachelor of Hospitality and Tourism (BHT), Electronics and Communications Technology (ECT), and Education (EDUC) programs. The event included a Unity Dance, Band Exhibition, and Cheer Dance Competition, all of which were met with enthusiastic support from students, faculty, and the community. The Unity Dance was held to celebrate the diversity and unity of Fullbright College's student body. Students from various courses and backgrounds came together to perform a choreographed dance that highlighted their unique cultural traditions and dance styles. The audience cheered and applauded as the students showed off their moves and celebrated their unity. The Cheer Dance Competition featured teams from different courses in BHT, ECT, and EDUC programs. The teams competed with high-energy routines that included stunts, jumps, and dance moves. The audience was impressed with the skills and coordination displayed by the teams, and the competition was intense until the very end.

Sports

Fullbright College's Football Game at Palawan Sports Complex Concludes with Criminology Team Taking the Championship


Fullbright College recently held its intramurals 2023 football game at the Palawan Sports Complex, where students from different courses competed for the championship title. After a series of intense games, the Criminology team emerged as the champions, with the Electronics and Communications Technology (ECT) team coming in second place and the Education (EDUC) team in third place. The football games were fiercely contested, with each team displaying their skills and determination on the field. The Criminology team, in particular, demonstrated an excellent understanding of tactics and teamwork that allowed them to outmaneuver their opponents and score crucial goals. The ECT and EDUC teams also put up a strong fight, but they were unable to match the Criminology team's overall performance. Fullbright College's intramurals 2023 football game was a showcase of the talents and skills of its students, and it was a great opportunity for them to come together and compete in a friendly and competitive environment. The college is proud of all the teams who participated and congratulates the Criminology team on their championship win.

    Intramurals 2023 Results:

  • Champion: Criminology
  • 1st Place: Electronics and Communications Technology
  • 2nd Place: Education
  • 3rd Place: Bachelor of Hospitality and Tourism

Fullbright College's Girls' Basketball Tournament in Palawan Sports Complex Ends with Criminology Taking Home the Championship


Fullbright College recently hosted its Girls' Basketball Tournament as part of its Intramurals 2023. The tournament was held in Palawan Sports Complex, and it brought together students from different courses to compete for the championship title. After several intense games, the Criminology team emerged as the champions, followed by the Bachelor of Hospitality and Tourism (BHT) team in second place and the Education (EDUC) team in third place. The tournament saw some impressive performances from the participating teams. The Criminology team played with great energy and skill throughout the tournament, winning their games with ease. The BHT team also displayed their excellent teamwork and played some exciting games, but they were unable to overcome the dominance of the Criminology team. The EDUC team also performed admirably, and their efforts were rewarded with a third-place finish. Fullbright College's Girls' Basketball Tournament was a great opportunity for students to showcase their skills and passion for basketball. The college is proud of all the teams who participated and congratulates the Criminology team on their championship win.
